Welcome to the Christmas 2022 game

This year's game is a cooperative multiplayer cross between Zuma and Bejewelled for up to four players.

In every level, the players take one of the following roles:

  • Pitcher: controls the gun that can shoot bullets/jewels that can be then caught by catchers. The gun is on the very end of the LED chain, the fourth led is the jewel/bullet that will be fired, the other three have colours of the Xbox 360 controller buttons B, X and Y and you have to press the right buttons in sequence to reload the gun to another jewel type.

    You can always fire the gun with A, it is reloaded automatically by a bullet of the same type.

    Pitcher does not and cannot move, it is not displayed on the playing field.

  • Catcher: moves around the chain by left stick or D-pad. He or she can catch a bullet when it is flying by and turn it into jewel by pressing A. If the new jewel becomes part of a chain of at least three jewels of the same type, all these jewels are destroyed. Did we mention that destroying all jewels is goal of the game?

    Catchers don't have any other controls, they have to work closely with the pitcher to get the right bullets at the right place.

    Catchers are displayed as white LEDs when moving and as blinking LEDs whe stopping.

  • Switcher: roams around the LED chain and can swap the two neighbouring jewels in an effort to create a chain of at least three jewels of the same type. Press B to swap to the right of your position and press X to swap to the left of it. If there is no match, the jewels will return to their original positions quickly.

    The Switcher moves and looks just like Catchers.

The objective of the game is to destroy all jewels. There are several levels, with increasing difficulty, and one final level that may reveal interesting information. You might find 'Cipher Aids' handy.

Note about movement:

  • You can move by left stick or D-pad.
  • Pressing Right Shoulder Button will highlight your position on the chain and hide all other players.
  • Pressing Left Soulder button will instantly teleport you to the end of the chain, just behind the gun.
